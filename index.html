Dr. Evan Johnson is a researcher with the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with over ten years of experience in quantitative research and policy analysis. He has won NSF grants to study government incentives for innovation and small businesses.

Dr. Johnson and his team are skilled in data analytics, data visualization, mapping, and programming. Their work benefits clients in the public, private, and academic sectors looking to solve problems with new data and rigorous analytical tools. Specific areas of expertise include program evaluation and causal inference; data visualization; business data; government incentives for innovation; entrepreneurship and strategy research.
